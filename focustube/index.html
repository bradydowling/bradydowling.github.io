Features

With YouTube, less is more. FocusTube enhances YouTube by removing distractions and giving you control of your experience.

Hidden Comments

Nothing distracts from a YouTube video like comments. This Chrome extension hides comments by default so you can focus on the video content.

Clean Homepage

The homepage on YouTube is constantly begging you to jump into all kinds of videos. This proves a distraction so FocusTube blanks it out.

No Recommendations

Personalized recommendations are fun, but also terribly distracting. To help you focus, FocusTube hides YouTube's recommendations.

Autoplay Turned Off

No more hurrying to stop YouTube from automatically playing that next related video. Now you're in control of what videos you watch and how long you watch them.

Child Friendly

Don't worry about your kids seeing inappropriate recommendations, comments, or related videos. FocusTube hides them all for a kid friendly YouTube.

Customizable

If you're missing some of YouTube's best distractions, just selectively disable FocusTube features to get the perfect viewing experience for you.
